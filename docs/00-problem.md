# Problem

Construction firms and small contractors rent equipment using manual processes, resulting in poor tracking of inventory, contracts, and collections.

## Target User
- Small and medium-sized contractors
- Local construction equipment rental companies

## Current Pain Points
- No clear visibility of available inventory
- Poorly managed rental contracts
- Manual collections and frequent errors
- Equipment loss and operational conflicts

## What this system does NOT solve (Out of Scope)
- Full fiscal accounting
- Equipment sales
- Payroll management
